Introduction

I was 10 when I wrote my first computer program. In the dark basement tech lab of my school, the Iron Curtain still in place, I made a Soviet IBM-knockoff play The Beatles' Yellow Submarine.

The teacher told me I was cute, but coding was for boys. It was what everyone else was telling me about math, and chess, and RPGs, and so I believed him, and it took me the longest time to find my way back here.

So hi! Hello! I'm Cam, and I wrote this in HTML. With some light CSS styling. And already it looks better than anything I've ever cooked, so there.
